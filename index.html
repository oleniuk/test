ABOUT US

At PlayLand Games, our publishing arm is at the cutting edge of the gaming industry. We don't just develop and publish games; we are dedicated to curating exceptional gaming experiences.

Our mission is to identify and uplift remarkable games, offering them a platform to captivate audiences worldwide. We collaborate intimately with developers, ranging from indie creators to well-known studios, guiding them through the intricacies of distribution,marketing, and user engagement.

Our commitment is to ensure that every game we publish reaches its fullest potential and resonates with players globally.

WORKING CONDITIONS

Modern Office

Our stylish office features a stunning panoramic view, cozy workstations, and comfortable chairs. The kitchen is stocked with light breakfasts, fruits, and sweets throughout the day, with delightful surprises every Friday! Check out more photos and details about our office on our Habr blog.

Core Values

We value the process as much as the outcome. We advocate for comfortable and creative working conditions, offering both in-office and remote work options. We foster an atmosphere of trust and ensure a high degree of team autonomy.

Exciting Projects

We are passionate about developing original projects that strive for AAA quality. With extensive expertise in mobile platforms, we are now actively expanding into console and PC game development. We create games that we ourselves love to play!

Cutting-Edge Technologies

Our teams utilize the best technologies, uphold a high code culture, and incorporate AI into our development processes. We employ top industry professionals who are committed to continuous learning and realizing their full potential.

Strong Culture

We emphasize transparent development, mutual assistance, and continuous knowledge exchange. Employee growth is a top priority, with many of our team members publishing articles and participating in conferences .

Hobby Clubs

We enjoy playing consoles, Magic the Gathering, and board games, watching movies, and analyzing different games. We frequently organize tournaments, practice karate, and play football. We are always happy to share and support your hobbies!
